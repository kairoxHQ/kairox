# Principles

- The system is broker-agnostic.
- Broker integrations must use adapter interfaces.
- Risk checks must happen before any execution.
- Every recommendation and trade must be logged.
- Every decision must be explainable.
- Live trading must remain disabled by default.
- Secrets must never be committed to GitHub.
- Users will eventually choose their own goals, risk level, and trading activity.
- The long-term objective is to grow net worth within the user's chosen risk limits.
- Do not optimize for trade frequency.
- Doing nothing is a valid decision.
